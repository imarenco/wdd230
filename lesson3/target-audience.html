Target Audience

The audience is people who want to visit Bariloche and want to see what activities can be done, due to the type of activities that there are generally couples or families, a group of people interested in hiking and biking trails also come.

Personas

Persona 1

The first person is called "Steve Patrick", he is 40 years old, who works as "Hiring Manager" in a Boston company, who is looking for activities to do on vacation with his wife and two children in Bariloche.

Persona 2

The second person is called "Luna Warren", she is 24 years old, she works as a "stylist", she wants to take a trip with a friend to Bariloche because I hear that it is a good place in winter to do snow sports.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

User who heard about bariloche and wants to know what kind of activities and excursions the place offers.

Scenario 2

User looking to hike in the woods.

Scenario 3

Lusers looking for which national parks they can visit.

Scenario 4

User looking for where he can do snow sports, like Snowboard

Scenario 5

User looking for kayak excursions in the lakes of bariloche.
